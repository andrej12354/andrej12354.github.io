Rules

Official rules of MineDime server

General Rules

1 No hacking, cheating, or using any illegal clients/mods that give unfair advantages.
2 No duping items or exploiting bugs. Report bugs to staff instead of abusing them.
3 No spamming, advertising, or disruptive behavior that ruins the gameplay experience.
4 Griefing and raiding are allowed as part of PvP gameplay; we do not protect builds.
5 No alt-account abuse or multi-accounting to gain unfair advantage.

Chat Rules

1 No swearing, harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.
2 No advertising or self-promotion in chat.
3 Preferred language is English, but other languages are allowed.
4 Respect all players and staff at all times.
